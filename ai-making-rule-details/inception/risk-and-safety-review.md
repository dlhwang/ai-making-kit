# Risk And Safety Review

Execute when a project involves sharp tools, heat, electricity, batteries, loads, wearable contact, moving parts, structural support, children, public use, or failure consequences.

## Analyze

- Fabrication hazards
- Assembly hazards
- Use hazards
- Material failure modes
- Electrical/thermal risks
- Sharp edge risks
- Tipping, falling, crushing, pinching, or slipping
- Device damage risks
- Mitigations
- Required tests

## Output

Create `making-docs/inception/risks/risk-and-safety-review.md`.

Include:

- Risk table
- Severity
- Likelihood
- Mitigation
- Validation method
- Residual risk
