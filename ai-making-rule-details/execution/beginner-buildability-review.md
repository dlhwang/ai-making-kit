# Beginner Buildability Review

Purpose: evaluate whether a novice can complete the build from the package.

## Analyze

- Can the novice identify every part?
- Are quantities clear?
- Are stacked layers clear?
- Are orientations clear?
- Are glue/fastener locations clear?
- Is there a test coupon?
- Is there a beginner layout?
- Are difficult cuts reduced?
- Is there a recovery path for fit failure?
- Are irreversible steps delayed until after fit checks?

## Output

Create `making-docs/execution/review/beginner-buildability-review.md`.

Include:

- Difficulty rating
- Success probability
- Likely failure points
- Required simplifications
- Required documentation fixes
