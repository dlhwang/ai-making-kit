# Quality Criteria

Purpose: define how making deliverables are judged.

## Criteria

- Dimensional accuracy
- Material appropriateness
- Fabrication feasibility
- Fit and tolerance realism
- Assembly clarity
- Structural adequacy
- Usability
- Safety
- Export validity
- Test coupon coverage
- Beginner buildability
- Documentation completeness

## Output

Create `making-docs/execution/quality-criteria.md`.

Use these criteria during Review And Validation.
