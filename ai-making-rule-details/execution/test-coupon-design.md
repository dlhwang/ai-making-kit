# Test Coupon Design

Purpose: create a small physical test before committing to the full build.

## Include As Relevant

- Material thickness slot
- Tab and slot pair
- Critical hole
- Critical corner radius
- Cable/connector pass-through
- Bend relief
- Adhesive sample
- Fastener hole
- Living hinge sample
- Print tolerance sample
- Angle gauge or alignment sample

## Output

Create or document a test coupon file/view and create:

- `making-docs/execution/tests/test-coupon-plan.md`

Include:

- What to cut/print
- What to measure
- Pass/fail criteria
- Parameters to change when it fails
