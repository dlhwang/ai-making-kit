# AI-MAKING Terminology

## Making Project

A physical design task involving CAD, drawings, fabrication, assembly, testing, or prototype iteration.

## Design Package

The set of files needed to build and validate an object: source CAD, exported cut/print files, specs, test coupon, assembly guide, and validation notes.

## Test Coupon

A small, cheap, early test artifact used to validate material thickness, kerf, fit, holes, tabs, slots, bend relief, and key interfaces before cutting or printing the full design.

## Fit Check

A geometry or physical test that verifies whether real parts insert, align, clear, retain, or move as intended.

## Fabrication Geometry

Geometry intended to be cut, printed, machined, folded, or assembled.

## Preview Geometry

Geometry intended only for visualization, such as dummies, transparent parts, or assembly-only overlaps.

## Beginner Buildability

The degree to which a novice can identify parts, understand quantities, assemble in the right order, avoid irreversible mistakes, and recover from fit problems.
