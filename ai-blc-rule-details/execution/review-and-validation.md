# Review and Validation

Purpose: verify that deliverables are usable and aligned with requirements.

## Validate Against

- Work requirements
- Execution plan
- Quality criteria
- Stakeholder scenarios
- Source materials
- Scope boundaries

## Review Output

Create `blc-docs/execution/review/review-summary.md`.

Include:

- Overall status: pass, pass with issues, or fail
- Requirement coverage
- Quality criteria results
- Risks and unresolved questions
- Recommended fixes
- Approval recommendation

Wait for explicit approval before marking execution complete.
