# AI-BLC Terminology

## AI-BLC

AI-Driven Business Life Cycle. A workflow for doing practical business work with AI while keeping human intent, review, and decision authority explicit.

## Work Item

The unit of business work being handled. Examples: a proposal, report, process change, operating plan, campaign plan, training program, meeting design, policy document, or analysis.

## Deliverable

The concrete output of the work. Examples: document, spreadsheet, slide outline, decision memo, meeting agenda, process map, checklist, report, email, project plan, or operating guide.

## Stakeholder

Anyone who requests, reviews, approves, uses, or is affected by the work.

## Acceptance Criteria

Conditions that must be true for the work to be considered usable and complete.

## Quality Criteria

Standards for accuracy, completeness, compliance, confidentiality, usability, timeliness, and communication.

## Operating Rhythm

The recurring cadence after delivery: follow-ups, reviews, metrics, status updates, retrospectives, and improvement actions.
