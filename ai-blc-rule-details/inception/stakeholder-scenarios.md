# Stakeholder Scenarios

Purpose: describe how different people will use, review, approve, or be affected by the work.

Execute this stage when multiple stakeholders are involved or when adoption matters.

## Steps

1. Identify stakeholder groups.
2. Describe what each group needs from the work.
3. Capture likely concerns and decision criteria.
4. Define acceptance expectations.
5. Identify communication needs.

## Output

Create `blc-docs/inception/stakeholders/stakeholder-scenarios.md`.

Include:

- Stakeholder table
- Scenario descriptions
- Acceptance expectations
- Communication needs
- Conflicts or tradeoffs

Wait for explicit approval.
