# Work Structure Design

Purpose: design the structure of a business artifact, process, policy, operating model, report, or governance routine.

Execute this stage when the work needs more than a simple document draft.

## Design Areas

- Artifact structure
- Process flow
- Roles and responsibilities
- Decision rights
- Data or evidence needed
- Review and approval path
- Communication model
- Maintenance model

## Output

Create `blc-docs/inception/work-structure/work-structure-design.md`.

Wait for explicit approval.
